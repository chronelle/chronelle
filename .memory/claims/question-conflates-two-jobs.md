# Question Conflated Two Distinct Jobs

The Question primitive did two unrelated jobs at once: tracking an *empirical
inquiry* (what is not yet known) and tracking an *undecided deliberation* (what
has not yet been chosen).

## Evidence

- When the atomic-vs-bundled matter arose, it was modeled as an open Decision,
  not a Question — the deliberative half already lived in Decision.
- Design analysis showed the empirical half maps cleanly to an `unsettled`
  [[proposition-is-durable-unit|Claim]], leaving Question with no distinct job of
  its own.

This Claim is settled: it is what licenses removing Question.
