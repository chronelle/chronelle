# The Proposition Is The Durable Unit, Not The Finding

The truth of a proposition has a lifecycle: `unsettled → settled → refuted`, with
the possibility of reopening. Therefore the durable unit is the *proposition
itself*, and "discovery" is the act of *settling* a proposition — an operation,
not a separate primitive.

## Evidence

- Findings recorded this session (the JSearch auth header; skill-casing splitting
  aggregates) were homeless propositions smeared into commit messages and Episode
  prose. They had no identity, could not be linked to everything they motivated,
  and could not sit unactioned.
- Modeling them as a stateful proposition node gives identity, linkage, and a
  lifecycle — and folds the earlier "Discovery" idea into a status transition
  rather than a new noun.

This Claim is settled: it is what licenses adding the Claim primitive.
