# Add Claim As A Truth-Apt Primitive

Chronelle adds Claim: a proposition whose truth the organization tracks, with
status `unsettled`, `settled`, or `refuted`.

Claim is the truth-apt counterpart to the will-apt primitives (Goal, Constraint,
Decision). A Decision is chosen and is not true or false; a Claim is settled by
evidence. Settling a Claim is the act of discovery — an operation, not a
primitive.

Grounded in [[proposition-is-durable-unit]].

See `ontology/claim.md` for the definition.
