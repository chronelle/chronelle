# Remove Question In Favor Of Decision And Claim

Chronelle removes the Question primitive. Its two jobs are reassigned:

- empirical inquiry ("what is not yet known") → an `unsettled` [[add-claim-primitive|Claim]]
- undecided deliberation ("what has not yet been chosen") → a Decision that has
  not yet been accepted

Alternatives that previously hung off a Question re-parent onto the open
Decision; the Alternative primitive itself is unaffected.

Grounded in [[question-conflates-two-jobs]]. Depends on [[add-claim-primitive]],
which supplies the home for the empirical half.
