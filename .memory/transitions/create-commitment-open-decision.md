# Create The Commitment Open Decision

## Rationale

Whether to unify the will-apt family is a real, unresolved ontology choice. It is
recorded as an open Decision rather than acted on, keeping the named variants in
use for now.

## Before

The unification possibility existed only as a settled structural Claim.

## After

Open Decision `commitment-as-fundamental-primitive` records the question, candidate
resolutions, and the decision to keep named variants for now.
