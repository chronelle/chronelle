# Create Actor Decision

## Rationale

Elevating Actor is a material change to the ontology and needed a recorded
decision.

## Before

Actor was an informal string field on Transitions and Episodes.

## After

Chronelle records the decision to make Actor a first-class durable primitive
representing participants, human or agent.
